月亮幻觉

Moon illusion
飞蛾把月光作为导航，但月光离地球很远。灯光出现后马上就变成飞蛾的导航飞蛾按本能仍然使自己同光源保持着固定的角度，于是只能绕着灯光打转转，直到最后精疲力尽而死/Moths use the moon as a guide, but the moon is far away from the earth. When the light appeared, it immediately became the guide of the moth. The moth instinctively kept itself at a fixed angle with the light source, so it could only revolve around the light until it was exhausted and died
