复眼/compound eye

复眼（Compound eye）是相对于单眼而言的，是昆虫的主要视觉器官，通常在昆虫的头部占有突出的位置。多数昆虫的复眼呈圆形、卵圆形或肾形。它由多数小眼组成。每个小眼都有角膜、晶椎、色素细胞、视网膜细胞、视杆等结构，是一个独立的感光单位。飞蛾的复眼是由2800个小眼组成，这样在光线微弱时，物体也能成像。

Compound eye is relative to monocular, which is the main visual organ of insects. It usually occupies a prominent position in the insect's head. The compound eyes of most insects are round, oval or kidney shaped. It consists of many small eyes. Each microphthalmos has cornea, crystal vertebra, pigment cells, retinal cells, rods and other structures, which is an independent photosensitive unit. The moth's compound eye is composed of 2800 small eyes, so that when the light is weak, the object can also be imaged
